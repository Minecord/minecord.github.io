Frequently Asked Questions

  • Is the bot 24/7?
    Yes, the bot is hosted 24/7 with little downtime for maintenance.
  • Why can't I use &purge?
    You need the Manage Messages permission.
  • Why doesn't &skin @b1nzy work?
    The bot uses Minecraft accounts to look up player data, not Discord accounts.
  • Can I link my Minecraft account?
    Sorry, but right now that's not possible. If enough people want to sync their Minecraft and Discord accounts then it'll be added.
  • What is an elevated user and how do I become one?
    Elevated users have access to bot administration commands like &say and &reload. There is no way to become an elevated user.
  • Can I self-host the bot?
    If you want to customize the bot for your specific server, check out the GitHub. There are loads of configuration options for anyone who wants to self-host on their own hardware.
